Privacy Policy · Last updated 13 June 2026

ARScale does not collect any of your data. Everything happens on your phone, and nothing is ever sent to us or anyone else. The app works fully offline.

Camera

ARScale needs your camera to measure things in augmented reality. The camera is used only on your phone, in real time. Photos and video are never saved, uploaded, or shared.

No accounts, no tracking

There are no sign-ups, no ads, and no analytics. Any settings you choose are stored on your phone only and are deleted when you uninstall the app.

Google Play Services for AR

ARScale uses Google Play Services for AR (“ARCore”) for its AR features. Your use of ARCore is also covered by Google’s Privacy Policy.
